Topic: Stock Market Basics

Investing in stocks allows individuals to potentially earn returns through capital gains and dividends. Market indices such as the S&P 500 and NASDAQ track overall market performance. Diversification across multiple sectors can reduce risk in a stock portfolio. The stock market is a platform where investors buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies.
